Interstate Data-sharing

The Interstate Data-Sharing feature enables State PDMP Administrators to designate which states are authorized to send prescription data requests to their PDMP and to restrict access from unauthorized states.

Only states that have executed a formal Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) can establish a secure digital connection via the RxCheck platform. These states must be designated as "Selected States" within the Interstate Data-Sharing portal by the State Administrator to enable bidirectional data-sharing.

States that have not finalized a formal agreement cannot participate in interstate data exchange through RxCheck. If such a state attempts to send a request, it will receive a system-generated message stating: "Access denied by disclosing state".

Within the Interstate Data Sharing interface, two lists are displayed:

  • Available Sites (left) — PDMP sites that have not yet been authorized to connect with the host state. Sites in this list cannot submit data requests.
  • Selected Sites (right) — States that have been authorized to establish a bilateral connection. These sites are permitted to send data requests and receive responses.

10.1 Select States for Interstate Data-sharing

  1. Click on the Interstate Data Sharing button, located on the left-hand side of the screen.
  2. Scroll through the Available Sites list and locate the site you would like to add.
  3. Click on the blue arrow button aligned with the desired site.
  4. Verify that the site has moved from the Available Sites list to the Selected Sites list. Note: Recently moved sites are not listed alphabetically and will appear at the bottom of the Selected Sites list.
  5. Click the Save button on the right corner of the screen to confirm and record your changes.
Note: If the change was implemented successfully, an alert will momentarily appear with the message, "Alert – Selected Sites Saved".

10.2 Deselect States for Interstate Data-sharing

  1. Click on the Interstate Data Sharing button, located on the left-hand side of the screen.
  2. Scroll through the Selected Sites list and locate the site you would like to remove.
  3. Click on the orange arrow button aligned with the desired site.
  4. Verify that the site has moved from the Selected Sites list to the Available Sites list.
  5. Click the Save button to confirm and record your changes.

10.3 MOU Worksheet(s)

The RxConsole supports a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) worksheet that states can populate and submit to potential partnering states.

Note: This is an MOU worksheet, not a contract.

10.3.1 Populate and Submit the MOU Worksheet

  1. Click on the Interstate Data Sharing button, followed by the MOU Worksheet(s) option.
  2. The MOU Worksheet(s) page is displayed.
  3. Click on the Fill New Worksheet button (if you have not completed a worksheet yet) or View Saved Worksheet (if you have previously completed a worksheet).
  4. Populate the following sections: State Administration's Information, User Validation Procedures, Data Access and Security, Data Elements for Request Submissions, Query Response, and Miscellaneous. Note: Fields with a red asterisk (*) are required.
  5. Click the Save button to confirm and record your changes.
  6. After saving, click on View Saved Worksheet button to view the saved MOU Worksheet.
  7. To edit the worksheet, click on the Update button.
  8. To share the worksheet with another state, click on the Share button.
  9. Select a state from the dropdown menu and click the Share Form button to send the form to the selected state.

10.3.2 Review and Respond to an MOU Worksheet Submitted to Your State

  1. Click on the Interstate Data Sharing button, followed by the MOU Worksheet(s) option.
  2. The MOU Worksheet(s) page displays records submitted by other PDMP States.
  3. Click on the View Details button to display the MOU Worksheet data submitted by the other state.
  4. Click the Cancel button to discard the MOU worksheet data.
  5. Click the Print button to print or save a copy of the MOU Worksheet.
  6. To agree to the MOU Worksheet, click on the Finalize button.

The MOU Worksheet record will be displayed with a status of "Finalized" for both PDMP States.
